Mutate '26

by µLearn Foundation

Mutate is µLearn Foundation's annual leadership training flagship — the event that shapes the next generation of campus leads across Kerala. The 2026 edition brought together 100+ campus leads from institutions across the state for an intensive two-day program designed to equip them with the systems, mindset, and networks to drive real impact on their campuses.

As a Core Organizer, I was responsible for end-to-end execution — from coordinating logistics and managing teams to ensuring the program ran with the precision and energy it demanded. Mutate isn't just an event; it's the mechanism through which µLearn's ecosystem sustains itself, and being part of building that was one of the most demanding and rewarding experiences in my leadership journey.
