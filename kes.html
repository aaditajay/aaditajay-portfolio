Kerala Esports Series

by All Kerala Esports Federation

The Kerala Esports Series is one of AKEF's flagship competitive gaming events part of their mission to build Kerala into a serious esports talent hub. The September 2025 edition brought together 10+ top teams from across the state at the Marriott Hotel, Kochi, for a professionally organized, high-stakes tournament.

AKEF has over 1,000 registered players across Kerala's 14 districts, and this series is where the best of them compete. As Volunteer Coordinator, I handled on-ground coordination keeping operations smooth so the competition could stay in the spotlight.

Host Organisation All Kerala Esports Federation
