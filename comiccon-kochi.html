Comic Con Kochi

by Comic Con India

The inaugural edition of Comic Con Kochi landed on February 28 – March 1, 2026 at AICC Angamaly, Ernakulam — Kerala's first-ever Comic Con. Presented by Maruti Suzuki Arena and powered by Crunchyroll, it drew over 5,000+ delegates across two days, uniting anime fans, cosplayers, gamers, and creators in what became a landmark moment for Kerala's pop culture community.

As Volunteer Coordinator, I managed on-ground volunteer operations — ensuring the right people were in the right places so the experience ran without a crack.
