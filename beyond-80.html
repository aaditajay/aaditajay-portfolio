Beyond:80

by The Purple Movement

Beyond:80 was a large-scale student-driven initiative by The Purple Movement, a community built on the belief that learning should go beyond syllabi, gatekeepers, and borders. The event brought together 200+ students and 50+ industrialists around frameworks for community-powered learning, blending youth energy with real-world industry perspective.

As a Core Organizer, I worked on the strategic and execution side, building the structure that held the event together and ensuring every moving part landed the way it was planned.
